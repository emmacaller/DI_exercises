BLACK WIDOW


Natasha Romanoff is the super-spy known as the Black Widow!
Trained extensively in the art of espionage and outfitted with state-of-the-art equipment,
Black Widow's combat skills are virtually unmatched.
One of S.H.I.E.L.D's most valuable agents,
she has carried out numerous black-ops missions and has recently been assigned
by Director Nick Fury to keep an eye on the Avengers.
